About Valley Bridge

We are two software engineers currently working in Silicon Valley, with over a decade of combined experience at top tech companies like Google and PayPal.

Our journey started in India, where we completed our undergrad degrees, before moving to the U.S. for our Master’s — one from Stony Brook University, New York, and the other from the University of Southern California, Los Angeles.

Having successfully navigated everything from university applications, internships, job hunting, to eventually building a life and home in the Bay Area, we wanted to create a practical, honest resource to help the next generation of Indian students do the same.

At Valley Bridge, our goal is to simplify this process — from admissions to career guidance — through real, lived experience.
